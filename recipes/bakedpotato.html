Baked Potato

Description

This a great side dish that will fill you up and give you much needed starches and nutrients. Yum!

Ingredients

  • 4 small russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher or sea salt, plus for seasoning
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 bunch chives (about 1-ounce)
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 slices bacon, cooked crispy, and crumbled, optional garnish
